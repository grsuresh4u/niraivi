The Niraivi Story

Craft born from
devotion

"A saree is not just fabric — it is six yards of memory, ritual, and belonging."

Niraivi was founded with a singular belief: that Indian textile traditions deserve the reverence of the world's finest fashion. We work directly with master weavers across Tamil Nadu and Andhra Pradesh, preserving techniques passed down through centuries.

Every thread is chosen with intention. Every border is negotiated with loom masters who understand that a saree is a living heirloom — not merely a garment, but a story that a woman wears into the world.

Artisan-Direct

We pay fair wages to over 60 weaving families across South India.

Natural Dyes

Plant-based colours that deepen beautifully with each wash.

No Middlemen

From loom to your doorstep — authenticity, uncompromised.

GI Certified

Our silks carry Geographical Indication tags for provenance.
